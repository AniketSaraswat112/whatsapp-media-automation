# How to Change Your WhatsApp Business Display Name

## Change in your WhatsApp Business Manager

To change your display name in Meta WhatsApp Manager:

1. Go to Business Manager and select your business.
2. Click the hamburger menu icon in the top left.
3. Click **WhatsApp Manager**.
4. Click the account that you want to change the display name for.
5. Navigate to the left-most menu and click **Phone numbers**.
6. Under the **Name** column, hover over your current display name and click the **Pencil icon**.

<Image alt="Updating Business Name" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/9699e14-Screenshot_2024-05-16_at_3.47.23_PM.png">
  Updating Business Name
</Image>

7. In the **Edit display name** section, enter a new display name.
8. Select **Next**.

> 🚧
>
> Your businesses can change the display names up to 10 times in a 30 day period. Once the 30 day period is up, a new 30 day period begins and your businesses can again change the display names up to 10 times in this new 30 day period.
