# Manage Business Profile

## Editing your WhatsApp Business Profile

Ready to give your WhatsApp business profile a makeover?\
Head over to the Profile section, just a few clicks away! Navigate to **Dashboard > Your App > Profile**.

Once you're there, you're in control. While filling out your profile details is up to you, we highly recommend boosting trust and engagement with your WhatsApp senders.
