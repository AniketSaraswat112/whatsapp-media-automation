# Timeline Activity

* Timeline activity on the WhatsApp page refers to a chronological record of events or actions, showing when messages were sent, received, or any other interactions that occurred over a period of time.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/3ac2db3-2024-05-24_18-57.png" />

* Enabling 'create timeline activity' allows users to generate a chronological record of events or actions on a platform, capturing key interactions such as message exchanges, file sharing, or other notable activities over a specified period
* Gupshup's WhatsApp Messaging feature facilitates sending messages through WhatsApp, allowing users to engage with their audience via the popular messaging platform.
