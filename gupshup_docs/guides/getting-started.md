# Getting Started

* To get started, you need to have a live WhatsApp for Business account with Gupshup.
* If you do not have a live WhatsApp for Business account with Gupshup, do not worry. You can get it TODAY.
* Please fill out this form to contact our onboarding team. We will get back to you and help you get started quickly\
  link: [https://www.gupshup.io/contact-us](https://www.gupshup.io/contact-us).

The extension is capable of performing the following actions in your Dynamics 365 CRM. The extension supports two types of messaging:

* Two way whatsapp messaging, Live chat .
* cloud flow\[Custom connectivity]

Gupshup's WhatsApp messaging extension will be available in the following modules:

* Contacts
* Leads
