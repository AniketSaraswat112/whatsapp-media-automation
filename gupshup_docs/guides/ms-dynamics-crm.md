# Microsoft Dynamic 365 CRM

In this guide, we will cover how to install, configure, and send WhatsApp messages from Dynamics CRM using our extension, [Gupshup WhatsApp Messaging for D365 CRM](https://appsource.microsoft.com/en-us/product/web-apps/gupshuptechnologyindiaprivatelimited1717507324467.gupshup?ocid=GTMRewards_WhatsNewBlog_gupshup_08292024).

The WhatsApp Messaging by Gupshup app integrates WhatsApp directly into dynamic 35 CRM, allowing businesses to communicate with their customers via WhatsApp and convert them into clients directly from the dynamic platform.

# Purpose

* The purpose of the **WhatsApp Messaging by Gupshup** app in Dynamics 365 is to provide businesses with a new channel for communicating with their customers. With this integration, businesses can send and receive messages directly within the Dynamics 365 CRM platform.
* This centralizes all customer communications within a single platform, allowing businesses to streamline their processes and reduce the need to switch between multiple applications. It ensures that all customer interactions are easily accessible and organized, leading to improved response times and more cohesive communication strategies. For customers, this integration offers a seamless and direct way to connect with businesses, enhancing their overall experience and satisfaction.
