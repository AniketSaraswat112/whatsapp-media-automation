# Onboarding Guide

Gupshup's WhatsApp **Embedded Sign-up** provides an easy way to register a WhatsApp Access API App for your business/brand. This process is self-service and can be started from Gupshup's Dashboard.

Registering a WhatsApp Business account will allow you to send and receive messages from your company's brand using Gupshup's APIs. Meta enforces certain restrictions and you will likely need to complete Meta Business Verification before making your App live.

## Prerequisites

Before you get started with this guide, you'll need:

* A Gupshup account.
* A valid email address.
* A new phone number. Alternatively, you can migrate a live phone number.

> 📘
>
> The number must be able to receive an SMS or phone call. If the number can only receive phone calls, it cannot be using an IVR.
>
> If migrating from the current BSP to Gupshup using the Embedded Signup Flow, disable 2FA for the phone number at the source BSP.

During this process, you can either create a new Meta(Facebook) Business Account or select an existing one. We recommend using an existing Meta(Facebook) Business Account if someone within your company has already created and verified it. If this is the case, they will either need to invite you as an administrator with full access or you can share the **Direct Facebook Registration**link with the Meta(Facebook) business account owner. They can log in and complete the WhatsApp Business Account process.

## Let's Get Started

1. Start by logging into your Gupshup Dashboard. Then, select **WhatsApp**

   <Image alt="Login > Dashboard > WhatsApp" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/288b8f3-Screenshot_2024-05-22_at_10.30.09_AM.png">
     Login > Dashboard > WhatsApp
   </Image>
2. You can either [create your first app](https://docs.gupshup.io/docs/quickstart-create-and-configure-access-api) or select an existing app from the dashboard.
3. Select **Go Live**. *You may see**Complete FB Registration** instead of **Go Live** if you have started the registration process in the past*.

   <Image alt="Go Live" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/d5166f7-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.38.11_AM.png">
     Go Live
   </Image>
4. Select the type of phone number you wish to configure for your WhatsApp Business Account.

   <Image alt="Select Phone No. Type" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/2e5010d-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.38.20_AM.png">
     Select Phone No. Type
   </Image>
5. Review the data storage region, the respective country's WhatsApp free, and select **Proceed**.

   <Image alt="Accept pricing" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/9269def-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.38.38_AM.png">
     Accept Pricing
   </Image>
6. Enter the required details and mark the checkboxes to agree with T\&C, Privacy Policy, Business Policy, and Commerce Policy. You will receive an OTP on the provided email address to proceed further. Enter the OTP and follow the prompts.

   <Image alt="Enter Details" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/e5f006f-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.38.57_AM.png">
     Enter Details
   </Image>

   <Image alt="Verify OTP" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/dbf9fc5-Screenshot_2024-05-24_at_1.06.50_PM.png">
     Verify OTP
   </Image>
7. Once your email address is verified, review your details and select **Confirm**.

   <Image alt="Preview" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/35a44e0-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.40.47_AM.png">
     Preview
   </Image>
8. Select **Continue with Facebook** to proceed further. *If you do not have the access to company's Facebook account, you can copy the URL and share it with your administrator*.

   <Image alt="Direct Facebook Registration" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/3eb2208-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.41.05_AM.png">
     Direct Facebook Registration
   </Image>

   > ❗️
   >
   > This step will open a pop-up window leading to Facebook.com. Do not close the pop-up window or navigate to a different page.
   >
   > Please ensure that your browser is configured to allow pop-ups to load.
9. Log into Facebook if you haven't already or click **Continue as\[your name]** if you're already signed in. After logging in, you will be shown an overview of the process of connecting your number to WhatsApp.

   <Image alt="Continue with Facebook" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/0a009d4-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.42.27_AM.png">
     Continue with Facebook
   </Image>
10. Select **Get Started**.

    <Image alt="Get Started" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/31ff3e7-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.42.37_AM.png">
      Get Started
    </Image>
11. Fill in your business information and select **Next**.

    <Image alt="Provide Business Information" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/9ea5aaa-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.42.51_AM.png">
      Provide Business Information
    </Image>
12. Next, you will be able to select a **WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)** and **WhatsApp Business Profile** or create a new one.

    <Image alt="Create or Select WABA" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/a97c23b-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.44.08_AM.png">
      Create or Select WABA
    </Image>
13. Now, provide details for your business information.

    <Image alt="Business Information Details" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/36d1e62-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.46.29_AM.png">
      Business Information Details
    </Image>
14. Create or select your WhatsApp Business account. Then, select **Next**.

    <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/2d2bbec-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.46.45_AM.png" />
15. Provide a WhatsApp Business display name and select the category. Then, select **Next**.

    <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/f9342b4-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.46.51_AM.png" />
16. Next, you will receive a verification code on your registered phone number. Enter the verification code and select **Next**.

    <Image alt="Enter Verification Number" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/09bf8bf-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.48.06_AM.png">
      Enter Verification Number
    </Image>
17. Review the permissions requested and select **Continue**.

    <Image alt="Review Permissions" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/492434e-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.49.17_AM.png">
      Review Permissions
    </Image>
18. You're all set to go. Select **Finish**.

    <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/f34fe57-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.49.30_AM.png" />
19. Confirm the WABA ID and phone number.

    <Image alt="Confirm WABA ID and Phone Number" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/9cc43f0-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.51.12_AM.png">
      Confirm WABA ID and Phone Number
    </Image>
20. Congratulation! You are now ready to send messages. Select **Go to dashboard**.

    <Image alt="App is Live" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/3ef9ab4-Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_11.53.50_AM.png">
      App is Live
    </Image>
