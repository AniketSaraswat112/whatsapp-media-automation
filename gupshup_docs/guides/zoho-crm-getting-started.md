# Getting Started

Getting Started with Gupshup WhatsApp Messaging for Zoho CRM

To get started, you need to have a Live WhatsApp for Business account with Gupshup.\
**NOTE**: If you have already registered on gupshup.io earlier, please skip the Step 0 and proceed to Step 1.

## Step 0: Get a Live WhatsApp for Business Account with Gupshup

If you do not have a live WhatsApp for Business account with Gupshup, do not worry. You can get it **TODAY** itself.

Please fill out [this form](https://www.gupshup.io/contact-us) to contact our onboarding team.\
We will get back to you and help you get started quickly.

The extension is capable of doing the following actions in your Zoho CRM. The extension will support two types of Messaging

* Two way whatsapp messaging, Live chat with Zoho contacts, Leads etc.
* Bulk messaging

Gupshup's whatsapp messaging extension will be available in following five modules.

* Leads
* Contacts
* Accounts
* Deals
* Invoices
