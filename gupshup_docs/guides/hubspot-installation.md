# Installation

Here are the general steps to install the **Whatsapp Messaging by Gupshup** app in your Hubspot account.

App installation can be broken down into three steps: Installation, Grant access to connect WhatsApp Messaging by Gupshup to HubSpot and configure your WhatsApp account

## Installation

1. Log in to your Hubspot account: To start with installation process, log in to your HubSpot account and then navigate to App Marketplace.
2. Search for the Hubspot CRM app **Whatsapp Messaging by Gupshup** : In the App Marketplace, search for the **Whatsapp Messaging by Gupshup** app and click on the app to view its details. use this  URL to install the app into any HubSpot Account :\
   **[Installation Link](https://app.hubspot.com/oauth/authorize?client_id=9bb29165-d9ae-4ec9-bb95-33142daf3fa0\&redirect_uri=https://prod-hubspot-crm.web.app/install\&scope=timeline%20files%20crm.objects.contacts.read)** /

<Image alt="search app in marketplace" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/11bdd3c-Screenshot_2023-02-06_at_8.24.11_PM.png">
  search app in marketplace
</Image>

3. Install the app: On the app's detail page, click the **Install** button to initiate the installation process. you may be asked to grant certain permission to the app.

<Image alt="install the app" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/7464398-Screenshot_2023-02-06_at_8.28.36_PM.png">
  install the app
</Image>

### Grant access to connect WhatsApp Messaging by Gupshup to HubSpot:

1. After installation, HubSpot application will redirect the user to a page to see a list of all your HubSpot accounts, Select the account where you want to install your app.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/30531cc-Screenshot_2023-02-06_at_8.38.51_PM.png" />

2. After choosing an account, you'll be presented with a list of all scopes.
3. Click **connect app** button to  grant access and authorize the connection .

<Image alt="connect app and grant scope acess" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/2b48a2c-image_4.png">
  connect app and grant scope acess
</Image>

4. After granting access, you'll be redirected to enter your Gupshup WhatsApp account details to configure and connect your WhatsApp account.

## Configure Gupshup WhatsApp account

* After installation, you'll be redirected to configure your Gupshup WhatsApp account details.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/eeb72a2-2024-05-24_11-33.png" />

* .After filling in all the details for the channel, whether it's for enterprise or self-serve, click **submit**.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/34d697d-2024-05-24_12-00.png" />

* If you don't fill in all the details for the channel, whether it's for enterprise or self-serve, and click the **cancel** button, the Gupshup extension will reopen the channel configuration screen.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/3aa3efe-2024-05-24_12-16.png" />

* After that, click on the contact page and create a new account or use an existing account for the configuration page, then reopen the Gupshup extension.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/b244781-2024-05-24_12-10.png" />

* For more detailed instructions on configuring your Gupshup WhatsApp account in Hubspot with the CRM app, please follow the instructions given on the following link:

[https://docs.gupshup.io/docs/configure-your-whatsapp-account](https://docs.gupshup.io/docs/configure-your-whatsapp-account)

> Note: If you fails to enter the necessary gupshup whatsapp account details during the initial installation setup, the app will open a screen to configure the account. you will be required to provide the necessary information in order to use the app.

* If you encounter any difficulties during the setup process, don't hesitate to reach out to the support team for assistance. [enterprise-support@gupshup.io](enterprise-support@gupshup.io)
