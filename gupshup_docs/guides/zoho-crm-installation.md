# Step 1: Installation

Now that you have a live Gupshup WhatsApp for Business account, let's Install the extension from Zoho App Marketplace.

* Navigate to [Gupshup WhatsApp Messaging for Zoho CRM](https://marketplace.zoho.com/app/crm/gupshup-whatsapp-messaging-for-zoho-crm) Extension on the Zoho App Marketplace and click **Install**

<Image alt="Search Gupshup WhatsApp Extension in Marketplace" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/419290d-Serach_for_Extension.png">
  Search Gupshup WhatsApp Extension in Marketplace
</Image>

<Image alt="Install Gupshup WhatsApp messaging for Zoho CRM extension." align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/8126f87-Click_on_install.png">
  Install Gupshup WhatsApp messaging for Zoho CRM extension.
</Image>

* **Confirm Installation** - Agree to **Terms of service and Privacy policy** of Gupshup , Agree to Terms of service of Zoho Marketplace and confirm Install

<Image alt="Confirm Installation" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/06de55e-Install_extension.png">
  Confirm Installation
</Image>

* Agree to the terms and click ***Continue***

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/7e1ae4a-continue_to_install.png" />

<br />

* Choose users/profiles that you want to install the extension and click ***Confirm***

<Image alt="Click on Confirm " align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/ad3cb22-click_confirm.png">
  Click on Confirm
</Image>

**Now Gupshup WhatsApp Messaging for Zoho CRM is successfully installed on Zoho CRM.**
