# Verify Your Business on Meta

> 📘
>
> [Meta Verified for businesses](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/308979828303560?id=579726174359330) is a paid monthly subscription that supports you across Meta platforms with features to help with discovery, impersonation protection, account support, and more.
>
> Learn how to [sign your business up for Meta Verified](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1381094345778877?id=579726174359330).

Not every business needs to go through business verification. You only need to verify your business if you want to access certain Meta technologies or developer features.

Learn more about [when to complete business verification](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1095661473946872?id=180505742745347).

## Prerequisites

* You can only start the verification process if your business portfolio is eligible for verification.
* If you don’t see the **Start verification** button in the Security Center but believe your business needs to be verified, confirm that [the product or feature you’re trying to access requires business verification](https://www.facebook.com/micro_site/url/?click_from_context_menu=true\&country=IN\&destination=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fbusiness%2Fhelp%2F1095661473946872\&event_type=click\&last_nav_impression_id=0sLfv1qKbC7ngAriJ\&max_percent_page_viewed=28\&max_viewport_height_px=873\&max_viewport_width_px=1920\&orig_http_referrer=https%3A%2F%2Fdocs.gupshup.io%2F\&orig_request_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fbusiness%2Fhelp%2F2058515294227817%3Fid%3D180505742745347\&primary_cmsid=2058515294227817\&primary_content_locale=en_US\&region=apac\&scrolled=true\&session_id=1kdkwquR4rlpbcIXM\&site=fb4b\&extra_data\[view_type]=v3_initial_view\&extra_data\[site_section]=help\&extra_data\[placement]=%2Fbusiness%2Fhelp%2F2058515294227817). If you received a notification or email saying you need to verify your business, you may refer to that for instructions on how to get started.
* You must have full control of the business portfolio.
* If you provide false or misleading information during the verification process, attempt to claim and verify a business you’re not authorized to represent, or otherwise attempt to circumvent Meta’s verification review system, Meta may prevent your business from being verified, remove your business's verified status or take additional action on the account.

> 📘
>
> The Meta Business Account, sometimes called a Business Manager account, was renamed. It's now known as a business portfolio.

## How to verify your business

* Go to Business Manager’s Security Center. Click **Start verification**.
* Provide your business’s details, including the legal business name, address, phone number and website.
* Confirm your business’s details.
* Choose a method to confirm your connection. A confirmation code will be sent if you choose email, phone, text message or WhatsApp.
* When you're finished, click **Done**.
