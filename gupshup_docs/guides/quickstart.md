# Create Your First App

In this quickstart, you will learn how to create and configure your first **Access API** app via Gupshup's WhatsApp Self-Serve Platform.

## Prerequisites

* A compatible web browser.
* A Gupshup account. *If you do not have an account with us, you can create it from[here](https://www.gupshup.io/auth/login)*.

> 📘
>
> You can create only one Gupshup user account with an email address.

## Create Your First Access API App

* Visit [Gupshup's WhatsApp Self-Serve](https://www.gupshup.io/auth/login) webpage and log in with your credentials. Alternatively, you can sign in using your Google, Facebook, or GitHub account.

<Image alt="WASS webpage" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/08a11da-Screenshot_2024-05-22_at_10.28.46_AM.png">
  Login with your credentials
</Image>

* Navigate to the top menu bar and hover over the **Dashboard** to open the drop-down menu. Then, select **WhatsApp**.

  <Image alt="Dashboard> WhatsApp" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/34d6561-Screenshot_2024-05-22_at_10.30.09_AM.png">
    Dashboard> WhatsApp
  </Image>

* Select **Create your first app**.

  <Image alt="Create App" align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/8e7eb56-Screenshot_2024-05-22_at_10.31.58_AM.png">
    Create App
  </Image>

* Enter a unique name for your WhatsApp Messaging App and **Submit** it.

* Your App is created successfully. Select **Go to Dashboard**.

> 🚧
>
> The app name should be a minimum of six characters long and cannot contain any space or special characters.
>
> This app's name does not reflect on your WhatsApp Business API. It is for your reference and should be unique to our platform.

<br />

> 📘 App Deletion
>
> Any app which has been created and not used in past **60 days** will be deleted by our system. For any queries reachout to [devsupport@gupshup.io](mailto:devsupport@gupshup.io)
