# Step 2: Authorisation

Once the app is successfully installed, we need to authorize the extension to work in Zoho CRM.

1. Once you install extension you will get below pop up on the screen.  Click on **Authorize** button.

<Image alt="Authorize Extension " align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/0c7a628-click_Authorize.png">
  Authorize Extension
</Image>

2.Select / login with your Zoho account.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/57c0d02-Enter_Email_id.png" />

3.Click on **Next** button.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/41cf7b8-Click_on_Next.png" />

4.Click on **Accept** button.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/6e4b548-Accept.png" />

5.Click on **Finish** button.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/dc7c239-Click_Finish.png" />
